I enjoy communicating how observations of galaxies connect to the origin of the chemical elements. My research sits at the intersection of telescopes, stellar physics, and galaxy evolution, making it a natural bridge between visible astronomical images and the physical processes that shape the Universe.

## Themes

- How telescopes such as ALMA and JWST reveal the chemistry of distant galaxies
- Why molecular lines and elemental abundances preserve galaxy histories
- How stars produce and return elements to the interstellar medium
